Announcing the Open Data Streaming Program
We're giving back to the community and enabling data producers to distribute open streaming data for free on Ably’s Data Stream Exchange (DSX) platform
Read our blog announcement
The Open Data Streaming Program aims to liberate the many sources of realtime data around the world. Collaboration in the developer community already produces amazing results, and by providing a platform for open streaming data, we hope to unleash even greater levels of innovation and collaboration.
If you produce open streaming data, the Program empowers you to:
  • Publish your data, without needing to think about infrastructure, protocols, APIs or how many consumers there might be
  • Push this realtime data to any number of consumers as soon as it becomes available, using the consumer’s chosen means of delivery
Do you want to make your open realtime data available freely? Or would you like to use one of the open realtime streams that are available now, including transport, weather, sport and financial data? Find out more about the Open Data Streaming Program.
 
The Ably Hub: The home of open streaming data
The Ably Hub is our app-store-like portal for realtime data streams. It makes it easy for developers and other data consumers to find realtime data and build innovative applications around it. All data hosted with Ably via the Open Data Streaming Program will be available for free via The Ably Hub, alongside other commercial datasets.
There’s already a broad range of streaming data available on the Ably Hub, including:
  • London transport information from Transport for London
  • Betting stats from public sports betting markets, and a range of other sports data
  • Predictions from PredictIt
  • News articles from HackerNews and Reddit
  • Bitcoin price and information from Coinbase and BitFlyer
  • Financial information from IEX Trading
  • Weather data from Open Weather Map
And we're adding new sources all the time. Please get in touch if you want to suggest a new source.
